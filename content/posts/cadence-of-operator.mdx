We bias toward clear inputs, time-boxed work, and measurable outcomes. **Cadence beats intensity**—and it keeps execution consistent when markets get loud.

## Daily rhythm that keeps the desk sharp

- **Morning:** Scan markets, review alerts, clarify priorities.
- **Execution block (2–4 hours):** Deep work inside preset windows—no context switching.
- **Review:** Log outcomes, update dashboards, schedule the next day’s focus.

## Weekly cycle to keep alignment

- **Monday:** Set the week’s priorities and align on trading or project plans.
- **Mid-week:** Ops review—website metrics, trading performance, key KPIs.
- **Friday:** Close-out, performance review, and adjustments for the next sprint.

> **Operator reminder:** Consistency beats heroics.

## Monthly cadence for strategic calibration

- **Week 1:** Financial close, P&L review, variance analysis.
- **Week 2–3:** Strategic projects and system improvements move the business forward.
- **Week 4:** Plan the upcoming month, align resources, and lock critical deadlines.
